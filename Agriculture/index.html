* Crop diseases


* Tips to Prevent Disease:


1.Sterilize seed-sowing equipment.
2.Clean tools after using on individual plants.
3.Keep pots well watered to prevent the compost from drying out, as desiccated crops are vulnerable to diseases. Where crops, such as zucchini, are susceptible to fungal 4.diseases, water the soil rather than the leaves.
5.Cut out diseased growth promptly to prevent the infection spreading, and remove any damaged growth, which may provide an entry point for diseases.
